Portfolio Builder

Step-by-Step Guide to Build Your Portfolio

Step 1: Download the Template

Download the Excel template to start creating your portfolio. Follow the instructions in the template to enter your data.

Step 2: Upload Your Completed File

Once you've completed the template, upload it here. Your data will be processed to create your final portfolio file.

Step 3: Save Your Portfolio

After uploading, your final portfolio file will be generated. Make sure to save this file to use in the calculator.
